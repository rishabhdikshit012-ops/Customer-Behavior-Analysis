# 📊 Customer Behavior Analysis

## Overview

This project presents an end-to-end **Customer Behavior Analysis** using **Python, MySQL, and Power BI**. It demonstrates the complete data analytics workflow—from cleaning and preparing raw data to performing SQL-based business analysis and building an interactive dashboard to uncover actionable insights.

---

## Dataset

The dataset contains customer purchase records, including:

- Customer Demographics
- Product Information
- Purchase Amount
- Review Ratings
- Subscription Status
- Shipping Type
- Discounts
- Payment Method
- Purchase History

---

## Tools & Technologies

- **Python** (Pandas, NumPy)
- **MySQL**
- **SQLAlchemy & PyMySQL**
- **Power BI**
- **Jupyter Notebook (VS Code)**

---

## Project Workflow

- Loaded the dataset using **Pandas**
- Cleaned and preprocessed the data
- Performed feature engineering
- Imported the cleaned dataset into **MySQL**
- Executed SQL queries to answer business questions
- Built an interactive **Power BI Dashboard**
- Generated actionable business insights through data visualization

---

## Business Questions Answered

- Revenue generated by male vs. female customers
- Customers who used discounts but spent above the average purchase amount
- Top 5 highest-rated products
- Average purchase amount by shipping type
- Spending comparison between subscribers and non-subscribers
- Products with the highest discount rates
- Customer segmentation based on previous purchases
- Top 3 most purchased products within each category
- Relationship between repeat purchases and subscriptions
- Revenue contribution by age group

---

## Dashboard

The interactive Power BI dashboard provides insights into customer purchasing behavior through:

- **KPIs**
  - Total Customers
  - Total Revenue
  - Average Purchase Amount
  - Average Review Rating

- **Interactive Filters**
  - Gender
  - Subscription Status
  - Category
  - Age Group

- **Visualizations**
  - Sales by Category
  - Revenue by Category
  - Customer Distribution by Subscription Status

---

## Key Insights

- Clothing generated the highest sales and revenue.
- Most customers are non-subscribers.
- Customer purchasing behavior varies across different age groups and product categories.
- Subscription status influences overall customer spending patterns.
